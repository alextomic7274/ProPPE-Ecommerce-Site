About Us

Welcome to PROPEE, your reliable online store for personal protective equipment (PPE). Based in Cork, Ireland, our company was founded in 2020 to provide high-quality PPE to individuals and businesses in need of dependable safety solutions.


The vision of PROPEE emerged in response to the global pandemic, when the demand for reliable PPE skyrocketed. Our founders, a group of passionate professionals with extensive experience in health and safety, recognized the urgent need for accessible, trustworthy PPE products. Driven by a commitment to protect and empower our community, PROPEE was born.


At PROPEE, we pride ourselves on offering a wide range of PPE products, including face masks, gloves, hand sanitizers, protective clothing, and more. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that our customers have access to high-quality PPE that meets or exceeds industry standards. We work closely with trusted manufacturers and rigorously test our products to ensure they provide the best possible protection.


Our user-friendly online store allows you to quickly and easily browse our extensive selection of PPE, ensuring that you can find the right products for your needs. With fast shipping and exceptional customer service, we aim to make your experience with PROPEE as seamless and stress-free as possible.


Thank you for choosing PROPEE as your trusted PPE provider. We look forward to serving you and helping keep you and your loved ones safe in these unprecedented times. Together, we can make a difference and promote a safer, healthier future for all.
